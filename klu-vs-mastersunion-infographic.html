02The verdict

What it all means.

Bottom line

KLU has the better skilling system; Masters’ Union has the better proof.

✓ Where KLU wins

A structured, almost-free, job-mapped certification ladder across 18 tracks and 16 roles, on top of outcome-mapped academics and a 420-hour placement engine. Masters’ Union runs no certification programme at all — this is a genuine KLU advantage to publicise.

△ Where KLU trails

The capstones are simulations and industry exposure is mostly guest lectures. Masters’ Union converts learning into a ₹33L signal through a ₹5 Cr fund, live CXO projects, a build-to-Demo-Day venture ladder, founder stipends, and CXOs who teach.

The move: keep the certification lead and bolt a real experience layer onto it — turn three or four capstones into live GCC-corridor projects, add a graded venture track, stand up a small student investment pool, and bring practitioners in to teach and sign off the capstones. KLU doesn’t need MU’s price tag; it needs MU’s proof layer on a skilling system it already does better.
